Story

Long ago, the lands of the old faith were not ruled by 4 bishops, but instead by hundreds of gods. Even Haro does not know what happened to them. However, after them came 5 powerful beings:

Leshy; chaos, Heket; famine, Kallamar; pestilience, Shamura; war, and Narinder; death

While Narinder represented the only constant, he was the only one of the 5 who wanted change and have the meaning of death changed. Leshy , youngest of the five has his eyes lost. Heket, with her throat cut neat. Kallamar's ears, torn from his head. And Shamura's skull was split. These represents "see no evil, speak naught, hear nothing and think none" as The One Who Waits made it so. The 4, enraged, created 4 chains, each bound to one of their lives, and chained Narinder.

One day, Shamura recieved a prophecy that a lamb would free Narinder. Narinder began sending vessels with his Red Crown to kill the bishops and start a cult in his name, however, none of them could kill even a single bishop and the bishops began sacrificing all the lambs in the land...

...Until one is left
